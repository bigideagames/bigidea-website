Delete Your Account

Superstar Hockey · Big Idea Games Inc.

You can request deletion of your Superstar Hockey account and all associated personal data at any time. This page explains what gets deleted and how to submit your request.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your progress, purchases, and data will be removed.

What Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, we will permanently remove:

  • Your player profile and username
  • All game progress, stats, and achievements
  • In-game currency and items
  • Tournament history and leaderboard entries
  • Any personal data associated with your account

What We Retain

We may retain certain data for a limited period as required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as:

  • Transaction records (for financial and legal compliance)
  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics data that cannot identify you

How to Request Deletion

Send an email to our support team with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Please include your in-game username or the email address associated with your account so we can locate it.

We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation once complete.

Ready to delete your account? Email us at:

support@bigidea.games

Include your username or account email in the message.
